Abstract

Surgery has evolved from basic techniques to highly sophisticated robotic-assisted procedures. This transformation reflects advancements in medical knowledge, anesthesia, sterilization, imaging technologies, and robotics. This article explores the journey of surgery through the ages, culminating in the advent of robotic technologies, highlighting the implications for future medical practice.
